About Me

Hello! I’m Elain, a self-taught visual designer and an aspiring front end developer.

I’m also a recent graduate of the University of Texas at Austin’s Didactic Program with a B.S. in Nutrition and a certificate in Business. Instead of pursuing a career in health, I decided that I wanted to make the dive into the intersecting realm of technology and design and create everlasting experiences.

Join me on my journey to becoming a more well-rounded designer and web developer as I challenge myself, document my process, and improve my craft.
